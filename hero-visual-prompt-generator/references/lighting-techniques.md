# Lighting Techniques for Premium Visuals

## Dramatic Lighting Styles

### Golden Hour
**Description:** Warm, directional sunlight simulation with long shadows and amber tones.
**Best for:** Food & beverage, lifestyle, organic products
**Keywords:** `golden hour backlighting`, `warm amber glow`, `sunset lighting`, `long dramatic shadows`
**Effect:** Luxurious, appetizing, natural warmth

### Rim Light / Edge Light
**Description:** Strong backlight creating luminous edges around subject.
**Best for:** Technology, beverages, cosmetics
**Keywords:** `strong rim lighting`, `edge-lit silhouette`, `backlit glow`, `luminous outline`
**Effect:** Premium separation, product definition, modern feel

### Volumetric Light
**Description:** Visible light rays passing through atmosphere/particles.
**Best for:** Dramatic reveals, luxury products, atmospheric shots
**Keywords:** `volumetric light rays`, `god rays`, `atmospheric lighting`, `light beams through haze`
**Effect:** Cinematic, dramatic, ethereal

### Split Lighting
**Description:** Half the subject lit, half in shadow for dramatic contrast.
**Best for:** Technology, fashion, bold brand statements
**Keywords:** `dramatic split lighting`, `half shadow`, `chiaroscuro effect`, `bold contrast`
**Effect:** Edgy, dramatic, high-fashion

## Studio Lighting Setups

### Three-Point Classic
**Description:** Key light, fill light, and backlight for balanced professional look.
**Best for:** All product types, versatile
**Keywords:** `professional studio lighting`, `three-point setup`, `balanced illumination`
**Effect:** Clean, professional, versatile

### Soft Box Diffusion
**Description:** Large diffused light sources for even, shadow-free illumination.
**Best for:** Cosmetics, skincare, minimal products
**Keywords:** `soft diffused lighting`, `shadow-free illumination`, `even studio light`, `soft box quality`
**Effect:** Clean, pure, premium minimal

### High Key
**Description:** Bright, low-contrast lighting with minimal shadows.
**Best for:** Clean/clinical products, minimal aesthetic
**Keywords:** `high-key lighting`, `bright even illumination`, `minimal shadows`, `pure white environment`
**Effect:** Clean, modern, approachable

### Low Key
**Description:** Dark overall with selective illumination for drama.
**Best for:** Luxury items, technology, premium spirits
**Keywords:** `low-key dramatic lighting`, `selective illumination`, `dark moody atmosphere`, `spotlight effect`
**Effect:** Luxurious, mysterious, premium

## Specialty Lighting Effects

### Neon / Colored Gel
**Description:** Colored light creating vibrant mood and energy.
**Best for:** Technology, gaming, youth-oriented products
**Keywords:** `neon [color] lighting`, `colored gel effects`, `vibrant [color] and [color] light`, `RGB accent lighting`
**Effect:** Energetic, modern, tech-forward

### Catchlights / Sparkle
**Description:** Pinpoint lights creating sparkle in reflective surfaces.
**Best for:** Jewelry, cosmetics, beverages with condensation
**Keywords:** `sparkling catchlights`, `diamond-like reflections`, `glittering highlights`, `jewel-like sparkle`
**Effect:** Precious, luxurious, eye-catching

### Translucent Backlight
**Description:** Light passing through semi-transparent subjects.
**Best for:** Beverages, gels, liquid products
**Keywords:** `backlit translucency`, `light passing through liquid`, `glowing transparency`, `illuminated from within`
**Effect:** Fresh, pure, appetizing

### Gradient Lighting
**Description:** Smooth transition from light to dark across frame.
**Best for:** Elegant products, gradual reveals
**Keywords:** `gradient lighting transition`, `smooth light falloff`, `gradual illumination shift`
**Effect:** Sophisticated, controlled, elegant

## Category-Specific Recommendations

### Beverages & Food
- **Primary:** Golden hour backlight, translucent backlighting
- **Secondary:** Warm rim lights, soft ambient fill
- **Avoid:** Cold/clinical lighting, harsh shadows on food

### Technology & Gadgets
- **Primary:** Rim lighting, neon accents, low-key dramatic
- **Secondary:** Soft box for material detail, colored gels for energy
- **Avoid:** Overly warm tones (unless brand requires)

### Cosmetics & Beauty
- **Primary:** Soft diffused, gradient transitions, gentle rim light
- **Secondary:** Warm flattering tones, sparkle catchlights
- **Avoid:** Harsh directional light, unflattering shadows

### Fashion & Lifestyle
- **Primary:** Natural daylight simulation, editorial split lighting
- **Secondary:** Warm ambient, golden hour mood
- **Avoid:** Flat industrial lighting, overly clinical

### Color Transitions

- `color temperature shifts from neutral to golden`

### Directional Transitions
- `light source moves from side to back`
- `spotlight tracks across subject`
- `lighting rotates around product`

### Mood Transitions
- `flat ambient transforms to volumetric drama`
- `clinical lighting softens to warm glow`
- `harsh lighting dissolves to ethereal softness`
